Shelley, Portland’s most devoted bicycle lover, shares how to truly care for your own bike. This book covers essential knowledge for any cyclist, including at-home repair techniques, bearing maintenance, tuning, and tips on navigating bike shops as a woman. Originally presented as a bicycle repair workshop at the Portland volunteer collective Zine Symposium in 2003, it is now reissued by Ningen Paper Press.
