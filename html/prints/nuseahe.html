Nuseahe, also known as Drawings for Tattoos by the artist Haesun Lee , is a collection of 89 small tattoo designs that can be engraved on skin. Haesun Lee creates dreamy drawings that evoke memories of daily life and habits, connecting to a world of imagination. As the drawings in Nuseahe puts the purpose for tattoos, they were portrayed with far more delicate and intimate lines, unlike the bold and sharp look of her original work.This book includes a conversation with the publisher about the drawings and the artist‘s own perspective.
